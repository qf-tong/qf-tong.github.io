Hi I'm Qingfeng!

I am currently receiving doctoral training at the Department of Linguistics and Communication, University of Birmingham, UK, supervised by Dr Marcus Perlman and Dr Gerardo Ortega. Before that, I studied at the University of Edinburgh in developmental linguistics with a shift to language evolution.

Research Interests

  • Perceptual modality: How have the differences in perceptual modality (e.g., visual vs. auditory) influenced the multimodal communication? Due to physical apparatus or the way we perceive the signals?
  • The evolution of writing systems: How did human writing systems, from ideographic forms, come to represent speech sounds?
  • Cognitive science：Language of Throught; Parallel Architecture; The origin of language...
