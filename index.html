Hi I'm Qingfeng!

I am currently receiving doctoral training at the Department of Linguistics and Communication, University of Birmingham, UK, supervised by Dr Marcus Perlman and Dr Gerardo Ortega. Before that, I studied at the University of Edinburgh in developmental linguistics with a shift to language evolution (supervised by Prof Kenny Smith).
